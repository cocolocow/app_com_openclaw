# Privacy Policy for Nod.i

**Last Updated:** March 2026

## Overview

Nod.i is a companion app for OpenClaw devices. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

Nod.i does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

### Data Stored Locally
- **Device configuration**: mDNS hostname, IP address, and authentication token for your OpenClaw device
- **Chat history**: Messages exchanged with your OpenClaw agent

### Camera Access
The app requests camera access solely for QR code scanning during device setup. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

### Microphone Access
The app may request microphone access for voice-to-text input. Audio is processed locally via the Web Speech API and is not stored or transmitted by the app.

## Network Communication

The app communicates exclusively with your OpenClaw device on your local network. No data is sent to third-party servers.

## Data Deletion

All locally stored data can be cleared via Settings > Disconnect & Reset.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project repository.
